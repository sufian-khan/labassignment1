MY favourite Quotations

Here are some of my favourite quotations from different Authors, Philosphers and Prophet(P.B.U.H)

  • The ink of the scholar is more sacred than the blood of the martyr.(Prophet P.B.U.H)
  • Four things support the world: the learning of the wise, the justice of the great, the prayers of the good, and the valor of the brave.(Prophet P.B.U.H)
  • To overcome evil with good is good, to resist evil by evil is evil.(Prophet P.B.U.H)
  • With faith, discipline and selfless devotion to duty, there is nothing worthwhile that you cannot achieve.(Muhammad Ali Jinnah)
  • Failure is a word unknown to me.(Muhammad Ali Jinnah)
  • Expect the best, Prepare for the worst.(Muhammad Ali Jinnah)
  • Nations are born in the hearts of poets, they prosper and die in the hands of politicians.(Muhammad Iqbal)
  • I have seen the movement of the sinews of the sky, And the blood coursing in the veins of the moon(Muhammad Iqbal)
  • When truth has no burning, then it is philosophy, when it gets burning from the heart, it becomes poetry.(Muhammad Iqbal)
  • The ultimate aim of the ego is not to see something, but to be something(Muhammad Iqba)
